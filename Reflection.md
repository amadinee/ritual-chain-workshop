# Reflection: Blind Auction in Bounties

Blind auction mechanisms create economic incentives for participation. Participants must commit RIT as bids, which are revealed later along with answers.

**Public vs Hidden:** Commitments and bids are hidden until reveal. Bids are public after reveal. Refunds are transparent.

**AI vs Human:** AI scores answer quality objectively. The owner finalizes the winner based on combined score.

**Advantages:** Creates economic incentives, rewards both quality and commitment, discourages spam.

**Trade-off:** Requires participants to risk RIT bids, but provides refunds for losers.
