# BlindAuctionAIBounty – Blind Auction with AI Scoring

This contract combines **blind auction** with **AI scoring**. Participants submit answers with hidden bids. Winners are determined by a combination of AI quality score and bid amount.

## How it works
1. **Commit phase**: Participants submit hashed answers with a hidden bid
2. **Reveal phase**: Participants reveal answers and bids
3. **AI scoring**: Owner sets AI quality scores
4. **Finalization**: Winner = AI score + bid amount
5. **Refunds**: Losers get their bids back; winner's bid goes to owner

## Why blind auction?
Creates economic incentives and rewards both quality and commitment.
